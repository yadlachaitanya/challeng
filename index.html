What is Yoga?

Yoga is an ancient practice originating in India that combines physical postures, breath control, meditation, and ethical principles to promote health and well-being.

Benefits of Yoga

  • Improves flexibility and strength
  • Reduces stress and anxiety
  • Enhances mental clarity and calmness
  • Supports cardiovascular and respiratory health
  • Improves posture and balance
